Learn About Colors

Why Color Combinations Matter

Perfect color combinations create visual harmony, evoke emotions, and improve usability. Designers use colors to guide user attention and express brand identity. Poor combinations can make content hard to read or off-putting.

Color Contrast

Contrast refers to the difference in luminance or color that makes elements distinguishable. High contrast is essential for readability and accessibility, especially in text.

Primary, Secondary & Tertiary Colors

  • Primary Colors: Red, Blue, Yellow
  • Secondary Colors: Green, Orange, Purple (made by mixing primaries)
  • Tertiary Colors: Red-Orange, Yellow-Green, etc. (mix of primary and secondary)
